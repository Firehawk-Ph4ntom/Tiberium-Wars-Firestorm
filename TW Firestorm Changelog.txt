                      TW Firestorm V1.09 Changelog

=====================================================

Global Additions/Removals:

- Updated all campaign missions (GDI, Nod, and Scrin) to include the latest mod changes. The campaigns are now integrated directly into the mod, rather than being a separate add-on
  > All Harvesters and Tiberium Refineries are cheaper and build faster
  > All Engineers move a little faster, and do not have a capture delay
  > All Cranes are able to build T1 and T2 defenses
  > All Tiberium Spikes and Tiberium Silos provide slightly more money

- Edited a bunch of UI elements:
  > The support power bar for Scrin now shows up in purple color (due to their new normalized purple HC for Scrin)
  > "New Intel Database" popup message UI box now shows up in purple color
  > Changed the color of the Command bar regarding unit stances and assigning certain unit commands from green to blue for GDI, and to purple for Scrin, as well as made the unit command bar semi-transparent
  > Changed the Radar HUD, build queue menu button elements, and selection detail boxes to purple for Scrin
  > Changed the "Sell", "Power Down", and "Repair" buttons as well as the build queue and selection boxes for GDI from green to light blue
  > The "veterancy"/"display upgrade"/"unit count" box elements under the selection box HUD for each unit are now semi-transparent
  > Slightly adjusted the visibility of energy meter's texture

- A new option in the Skirmish/multiplayer lobby menu has been added that replaces the standard crates option: "Enable PVE Game Balance"
  > Enabling the PVE Game balance option will result in the following:
    > All Harvesters and Tiberium Refineries are cheaper and build faster
    > All Power Plants give a bit more power
    > All Power Plants and Barracks Structures provide a small build radius
    > All Engineers move a little faster, and do not have a capture delay
    > All Cranes are able to build T1 and T2 defenses
    > All Tiberium Spikes and Tiberium Silos provide slightly more money
    > All Super weapon structures are cheaper and build faster

- Added new library maps for mappers, and adjusted the vanilla ones

- All faction's Cranes can now build a money crate, which can be used to help give money to allies in need

- A new 3-player co-op map called "[FS] Sarajevo siege" has been added

- The build queue limit for all production buildings has been increased from 99 to 999 (It's 2025, and we still can't queue more than 99 units? lol)

- Control group numbers will now appear in the player's house color
  > Adjusted the font size of the number to make it more visible

- 2 new music tracks from Tiberium Essence, "Fields of Green" and "Slow March" have been added to the Redzone music track list

- 2 new button click sound effects have been added for clicking active and disabled buttons

- The "Dark Blue" house color has been reverted back to its vanilla state
  > The "Purple" and "Light Pink" house color has been slightly edited

- All infantry healing structures can now heal up to 4 squad units at a time

- All flying aircraft transports can now heal injured infantry inside them, similar to ground transports

- All Wall Hubs and Wall Segments are now able to slowly auto-heal

- Crates now have an unfolding animation when picked up

- The "Commando" pip icon has been added to all epic units

- Beacons now have stealth to prevent them from being seen by opposing players

- The Ghost Stalker and Mutant Hovels now provide an aura buff to nearby allied Mutant infantry

- All Mutant infantry now heal from Tiberium-based weapons

- A new symbol has been added above vehicles to represent the slowdown effect from Tiberium crystals/Tiberium-based weapons similar to "Raged" and "Suppressed" symbols

- Added Tiberium Dust particle effects to GDI/Nod Tiberium Refineries, GDI Tiberium Processing Plant, and Nod Tiberium Chemical Plant when those structures are destroyed or sold

- The "Radar Scan" sound effect now plays when picking up Shroud-clearing Crates

- Added 3 new units in the Mutant Hovel:
  > "Tamed Tiberian Fiends": A new Tier-1 anti-tank unit. They consist of 3 members and are a bit stronger than standard Missile Squads. They do not use their bite attack, only their shard launchers
  > "Mutant Salvager": A new Tier-1 Engineer unit that moves faster and has more health than regular faction Engineers. They can also hijack enemy vehicles, repair Civilian Buildings, and even place Booby Traps
  > "Mutant Skirmisher Squad": A new Tier-3 artillery infantry unit. They consist of 2 members, their attack range is equivalent to GDI Behemoth Walkers, and they deal GRENADE damage type

- The Neutral Expansion Outpost, Neutral Tiberium Spikes, Tiberium Silos, Observation Posts, and EMP Control Centers now spawn a husk when destroyed

- Added a new shell map for the main menu along with new button layouts and a new mod logo!

- The Intel Database entries for the campaign have been edited to better suit the mod's narrative and content (**Note: More IDB entries will make their way into the mod soon, regarding KW stuff mainly)

- A unique Garrison model for Mutant Marauders has been added

- New infantry blood decal textures have been added

- Lowered the opacity of all radius decals for better visibility

- Walker husks (GDI Behemoth, Nod Purifier Warmech, Annihilator Tripod) are no longer mass selectable with other units

- The victory music for the Scrin faction has been changed to the alternate unused victory music EA made

- The model of Tiberium Crystals has been edited to look "Spikier"

- Fixed tree crusher weapons' AOE not being representative of the actual AOE of the weapons

- Fixed Beacons preventing build placement for enemy players if a player places down a Beacon in their base

- Fixed Spot and Beacon Bombard abilities not working when respective spotters were garrisoned inside a Civilian Building or Transport unit

- Fixed Harvesters not properly showing their proper Tiberium bar color when harvesting overlapping Tiberium crystals

- Fixed Tiberium Based weaponry spawning multiple Viceroids from one infantry kill

- Fixed Tiberium Veins' damage filter, now it will only kill medium, large, and epic unit vehicles, while not triggering attacks on hover units and light vehicles

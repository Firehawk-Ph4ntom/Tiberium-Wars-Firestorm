=========================================================================

                      TW Firestorm V1.11 Changelog

=========================================================================

=====================================================

Global Additions/Removals:

- Reworked the entire PVE GameMode System, it's now more refined:
  - No more UI Inconsistencies:
    > Refineries cost 8 Energy (Before UI still said it took 15, now it will display 8)
    > Power Plants provide 30 Power (Before UI still said it provided 20, now it will display 30)
  - Fixed Initial Capture Bonus of Tiberium Spikes and Tiberium Silos: ($750 Initial Capture for Tiberium Spikes and $1500 for Tiberium Silos)
  - MCVs are also now at $3500 when PVE Game Mode is enabled
  - Fixed Mutant Salvagers still maintaining their Capture Delay (Now they will instant cap enemy Structures)
  - Fixed Mutant Salvagers not benefiting from the x1.25 Movement Speed multiplier like other Engineers
  - Fixed Barracks' Structures (For GDI and Nod) replenishing Infantry Squads mechanic colliding with the way PVE GameMode was set up before to provide Build radius for those Structures. As a result, GDI Barracks and Nod Hand Of Nod can now replenish Infantry Squads again
  - Scrin Mastermind does not have Mind Control Capture Delay on Structures in this mode anymore, and is able to capture Superweapon Structures and all defenses
  - Neutral Tiberium Silos now provide build radius during this mode

- All Tiberium Field objects and Tiberium Fauna Objects are now see-through, meaning units can now shoot stuff over them directly instead of having to path around first

- Added 3 new Mutant units to the Dropship Bay!:
  > Looted Pitbull: Tier 1 Scout unit that is good vs. Light Vehicles and Aircraft. Has an Anti-Infantry/Anti-Structure Mortar, and can fire an EMP mortar round through an ability
  > Looted Humvee: Tier 1 Anti-Infantry/Anti-Aircraft unit, has a repair drone, and can garrison an infantry unit inside it. It is a great support unit, and has a mini-rocket pod to help with light Vehicles
  > Looted Mammoth Tank: Tier 3 Heavily armored unit. Akin to the Mammoth Tank, but with a garrison slot. This unit, while weaker, is much cheaper to produce and has decent crowd control capabilities

  - The only way to unlock and use these units for now is capturing the Dropship Bay with a Mutant Salvager

  - Adjusted the texture of all Missile projectile models depending on the upgrades that have been purchased ("Thermobaric Missiles", "Tiberium Core Missiles", "Blue Tiberium Catalyst")

  - Adjusted the OnDeath explosion FX for Subway Entrances

-------------------------

Global Balance Changes:

- The cost of all Cranes has been lowered from $1500 to $1300

-------------------------

GDI Additions/Removals:

- The Rig/Orca Rig will now maintain their Veterancy Ranks when Unpacking into a Battlebase/Orca Battle Fortress

- Added new textures to Repair Drones according to their healing filter to differentiate between them:
  > Yellow Repair Drone texture: Repairs Vehicles
  > Blue Repair Drone texture: Repairs Aircraft
  > Purple Repair Drone texture: Repairs Vehicles and Aircraft
  > Orange Repair Drone texture: Repairs Vehicles and Structures

- Modified the portraits of "Guardian MRT", "Orca Fighter", and "Advanced Battle Lab"

- Swapped the upgrade icons of "Rail Guns" and "Rail Gun Accelerator" upgrades

- Added a new texture to the Missiles of every unit and structure affected by "Thermobaric Missiles" upgrade if they have rocket mutions visible, and added visible rocket munitions to "Orca Figheters"

- Added new Selection animations to Tiberian Fiends

- Added HE Ammo SubObjects to the "Armadillo APC" and the "Hammerhead Gunship"

- The "Orca Airstrike" support power Orcas no longer benefit from "Sonic Weaponry" upgrade

Nod Additions/Removals:

- The "Disruption Tower" and "Voice Of Kane" Objects are now see-through, meaning units can now shoot stuff through them directly instead of having to path around first

- Added new textures to Repair Drones according to their healing filter to differentiate between them:
  > Normal Repair Drone texture: Repairs Vehicles
  > Black Repair Drone texture: Repairs Aircraft
  > White Repair Drone texture: Repairs Vehicles and Structures

- Added new voice lines to "Phantoms", "Cyborg Gunners", and the "Cyborg Commando"

- Added a new texture to the Missiles of "Attack Bikes", "Decimator Cyborgs", "Militant Rocket Squads", and the "Redeemer Warmech" when "Tiberium Core Missiles" upgrade has been purchased (As well as "Blue Tiberium Catalyst" upgrade)

- Added the "Garrison Clear" cursor to the "Cyborg Commando" when targeting Civilian Structures

- Added a Door model to the "Obelisk Of Light"

- Added the Propaganda Pulse effect and the "Emissary" "TV" model to the "Voice Of Kane"

  > The Emissary will now no longer show the "TV" mesh (The Slavik execution mini screen) during unpack
